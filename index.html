I’m currently a PhD student in Machine Learning at the University of Edinburgh. My supervisors are Dr. Charles Sutton and Prof. Chris Williams. Lately I’ve been working on generative modelling under corrupt or dirty datasets.

I have a BSc. and MSc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from IST in Lisbon,Portugal. I focused on Signal Processing and Telecom Systems in my Msc. Then I worked as Signal Processing Software Engineer for GNSS systems, mostly under a research project for ESA evaluating new techniques for receivers. I worked as a Research Assistant in Optimization applied to Image Processing, focusing on Proximal Algorithms. Lastly, I got a MScRes from Edinburgh University, with a project on Graphical Models for Integrity Constraints, and became a PhD student.
Interests. Machine Learning: Generative Deep Learning, Probabilitic Modelling, Variational Inference; Optimization; Signal Processing.
